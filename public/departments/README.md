# Department Logos

Place your department logos in this directory:

## Required Files
- **police-logo.png** - Police Department logo (recommended: 200x200px)
- **fire-logo.png** - Fire Department logo (recommended: 200x200px)
- **ems-logo.png** - EMS Department logo (recommended: 200x200px)

## Optional Files
- **police-badge.png** - Police badge/emblem
- **fire-badge.png** - Fire badge/emblem
- **ems-badge.png** - EMS badge/emblem
- **police-banner.png** - Police department banner (1200x400px)
- **fire-banner.png** - Fire department banner (1200x400px)
- **ems-banner.png** - EMS department banner (1200x400px)

All department logos support transparency (PNG format recommended).
